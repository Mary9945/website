About me

This is a website of my photography and my Nomadic Sewing Creations . As an archaeologist and photographer, I have the opportunity to travel and work in the Nlakapmux territory, part of my job as an archaeologist was to photograph not only wild flowers, plants, trees, animals, and land scaped/scenereies. Besides taking pictures for my job, i also enjoy taking family portraits, weddings, and photos of water, clouds. My other thing i enjoy is sewing. I make ribbon skirts, dresses and ribbon shirts of various sizes, traditional, fancy and jingle dress regalia.

Nomadic Creations

These photographs of reglia,(pow wow outfits) and bead work I do. I make traditional, fancy and jingle outfits; ribbon skirts, dresses and shirts, plus numerous other sewing projects such as moss bags, sleeping bags, capes for babies and small children. I work with both home tanned and commercial hide for making mocasins, bags, traditional buckskin outfits and i have some experince working with porquine quills, caribou and moose hair for tufting.
